Title:Kalakrithi

Description: Located in Banjara Hills in Hyderabad, Kalakriti Art Gallery is one of the finest and the most popular galleries in the city. The place has a beautiful decor and hosts large exhibitions frequently which attract a lot of art connoisseurs and tourists.

Title:Muse Art

Description:Muse Art Gallery is situated opposite the Hussain Sagar Lake inside the Mariott Hotel. The gallery was founded by Marriott and Kaali Sudheer Mopperthy and is a non-profit organization. It hosts several exhibitions to raise funds for the vulnerable and needy including funds.

Title:Shrishty Art

Description:Shrishty Art Gallery is situated in the Venkatagiri, Jubilee Hills in Hyderabad and is one of the most visited and renowned art galleries of the city.The gallery displays artworks and exhibitions by the contemporary, new artists. It also hosts displays.

Title:Birla Modern Art

Description:Housed in the premises of Birla Science Museum, Birla Modern Art Gallery, also known as Nirmala Birla Gallery of Modern Art boasts of a variety of art collections ranging from modern art to contemporary artworks from artists all around the world.

Title:ICON Art

Description:Situated in Banjara Hills, ICONART Gallery is another one of the most renowned and sought after galleries in Hyderabad. The gallery encourages new and budding artists and even has an art-curator and a critic who curates and organizes exhibitions.

Title:Gallery Space

Description:Located in MLA Colony in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad, Gallery Space is a dainty place popular for its stunning exhibitions and art works. The gallery promotes modern art and contemporary artists. It hosts several exhibitions and you are most likely to find one when you are visiting.

Title:Pegasus Art

Description:Jubilee Hills in Hyderabad is a hub for art galleries. Another one of the beautiful art galleries in the area is Pegasus Art Gallery which is a big hit and quite popular among the professional artists. The place is always budding with art connoisseurs visiting for the very stunning art exhibitions and displays.

Title:Alankritha Art

Description:Situated in Kavuri Hills in Hyderabad, Alankritha Art Gallery encourages the contemporary and budding artists by hosting umpteen exhibitions to display the artworks. The gallery hosts several solo artist and also group exhibitions.
